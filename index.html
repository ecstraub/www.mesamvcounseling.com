About

Your Guide on the Journey to Well-Being

Eric, LPC, NCC

Licensed Professional Counselor

As a licensed professional counselor, my passion lies in assisting individuals and families as they navigate life's challenges and discover their path to well-being. Born and raised in the Phoenix area, personal and professional ties to the community drive a commitment to supporting those who call this area home.

The therapeutic approach centers on you, creating warm, judgment-free spaces where clients feel truly heard and valued. Every person's journey is unique, which is why a custom toolkit of evidence-based techniques is drawn upon to meet specific needs and goals.

Flexible meeting locations are offered to best serve you and your family. Whether meeting at the Mesa office, in the comfort of your home, at your child's school, or another location that works for you, the commitment remains to making therapy accessible and comfortable.
